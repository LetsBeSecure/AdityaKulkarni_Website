Area of Research

My work spans the field of cybersecurity, with two core directions: strengthening web security through phishing webpage detection, and advancing network security via DNS security enhancements and privacy-preserving protocols. Beyond these, I explore AI-driven cybersecurity and cryptography, aiming to design defenses that are practical, resilient, and adaptive to emerging threats.
